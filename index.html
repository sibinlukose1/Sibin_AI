Selected work

Results, not roadmaps.

All case studies
01 HealthTech

Clinical notes were eating 3 hours of every physician's day

Problem

A 400-bed hospital system struggled with documentation burden — physicians spent more time on notes than on patients, accelerating burnout and cutting capacity.

What I did

Designed and deployed a fine-tuned LLM pipeline integrated directly into their EHR, trained on specialty-specific note patterns with a human-in-the-loop review layer.

Result
73% reduction in documentation time. Physician satisfaction score up 31 points.
02 Financial Services

Analyst team couldn't keep up with 200+ daily research reports

Problem

A mid-market asset manager needed to process and act on a flood of market research, but their team was overwhelmed and signal was getting lost in the noise.

What I did

Built a multi-agent research synthesis system with structured extraction, sentiment signals, and a daily digest routed to portfolio managers — live in 6 weeks.

Result
4× more reports covered per analyst. Coverage blind spots eliminated entirely.
03 E-commerce

Customer support was slow, expensive, and inconsistent at scale

Problem

A DTC brand with 2M+ customers was spending $1.4M/year on support while satisfaction scores stagnated. Agents were answering the same 40 questions repeatedly.

What I did

Led a 90-day AI sprint: intent classification, a RAG-powered resolution engine, escalation routing, and agent assist tooling — all trained on 3 years of ticket history.

Result
$680k annual savings. CSAT up 18 points. Resolution time cut from 14h to 2.3h.
